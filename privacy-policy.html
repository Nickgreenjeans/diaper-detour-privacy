Privacy Policy for Diaper Detour

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Diaper Detour ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

We collect your device's location data to:

  • Display your current location on the map
  • Find baby changing stations near you
  • Calculate distances and directions to stations

Location data is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently on our servers. You can control location permissions through your device settings.

2.2 User-Submitted Content

When you submit a review or add a location, we collect:

  • Your first name (required for reviews)
  • Star ratings (1-5 stars)
  • Written review content (optional)
  • Feature ratings (cleanliness, accessibility, etc.)
  • Business name and address (for manually added locations)

This information is stored in our database and displayed publicly to help other parents.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Payment information (the app is free)
  • Personal identifiers beyond first names
  • Device identifiers or tracking data
  • Browsing history or usage analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide core app functionality (finding nearby stations)
  • Display community reviews and ratings
  • Build and maintain our database of changing station locations
  • Improve the app based on user contributions

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Neon Database - PostgreSQL database with encryption at rest
  • Secure servers - Hosted on Render.com with SSL/TLS encryption
  • Limited access - Only authorized personnel can access stored data

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Foursquare API - To find nearby businesses and locations (no personal data shared)
  • Apple MapKit - For map display and navigation (location data processed by Apple)
  • Expo Location Services - For GPS functionality (part of React Native framework)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

  • Foursquare Privacy Policy: foursquare.com/legal/privacy
  • Apple Privacy Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy

6. Children's Privacy

Diaper Detour is intended for use by parents and caregivers (ages 13+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@diaperdetour.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain user-submitted reviews and locations indefinitely to maintain the app's community database. Users may request deletion of their specific reviews by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your submitted reviews and content
  • Request deletion of your reviews
  • Opt out of location services (though this limits app functionality)
  • Report inappropriate content

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

10. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to deletion. Contact us to exercise these rights.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
